FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Everything you need to know before partnering with Synchro.

How quickly can you find a tenant for my property?
+
Our average time-to-lease is 14 days from listing. For well-maintained properties in prime locations like Phakalane or the CBD, we've placed tenants in under a week. Our marketing reach and tenant database give us a significant edge.
What happens if a tenant stops paying rent?
+
We have a structured process: immediate contact on day 1, formal notice on day 3, and legal proceedings if unresolved by day 7. Our rigorous screening minimizes this risk, but when it happens, we act fast to protect your income.
Do you handle maintenance and repairs?
+
Absolutely. We coordinate everything from routine maintenance to emergency repairs through our vetted contractor network across Gaborone. You'll receive photo documentation and cost approval requests for anything above your pre-set threshold.
How do I track my property's performance?
+
Every owner receives a secure online dashboard showing real-time occupancy, rental income, expenses, and maintenance history. Professional and Enterprise plans include detailed quarterly performance reports with market benchmarking.
Is there a contract lock-in period?
+
We offer flexible terms starting at 6 months, with 12-month agreements receiving discounted management fees. We believe in earning your business every month — if you're not satisfied, you can exit with 30 days' notice.
